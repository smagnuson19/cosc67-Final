Help

How do I add items?

Use the slider arrows to navigate between different items on the main page and tap or click on the item to select it.

What payment types are accepted?

This system accepts both Dartmouth IDs (using DA$H or a Meal Swipe) and all major credit cards.

Where should I get my items?

Go to the counter for each item you've ordered. Locations of each item are inside the cafe.

How do I tell which item is mine?

Each item has an associated receipt underneath. Your name can be found on this receipt.
